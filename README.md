# 🔪 PeepoZax 🔪

A fun web-based game where you play as a Pepo with an axe, chasing and chopping down other pepos! Works on PC and mobile!

## 🎮 How to Play

### PC / Desktop
- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** - Move around
- **SPACEBAR** - Swing your axe

### Mobile / Touch Screen
- **Joystick** - Use the left joystick to move around
- **Attack Button 🔪** - Tap the red button to swing your axe

### Objective
Chase and slice the enemy pepos before they catch you!

## 📊 Scoring

- **+10 points** - For each pepo you chop with your axe
- **-1 life** - Each time an enemy pepo touches you
- **Game Over** - When you lose all 3 lives

## 🎯 Game Features

- ✅ Works on PC and Mobile
- ✅ Dynamic enemy spawning with increasing difficulty
- ✅ Smooth axe swing mechanics
- ✅ Collision detection for both attacks and damage
- ✅ Score tracking and lives system
- ✅ Touch controls for mobile devices
- ✅ Keyboard controls for desktop
- ✅ Pepo-themed characters with expressive eyes and mouths
- ✅ Responsive design (scales to any screen size)
